    • Pat Kenny’s 41-year high-profile career

    January 16, 2020January 16, 2020| Minnie ZookMinnie Zook| 0 Comment

    Patrick Kenny is an Irish seasoned presenter who is currently hosting Newstalk’s weekday radio program The Pat Kenny Program and Pat Kenny Tonight’s current events show on Virgin Media One. He had a 41-year high-profile career with RTÉ before this, in which he was their highest-paid host for several years.

    The tv and radio host carries on building a big nest egg for himself and his family. The Newstalk presenter has just reported a comprehensive set of accounts with an annual income of € 1,526,884 on his business, Pat Jenny media services.

    Accounts submitted with the Companies Office reveal that his firm’s earnings soared past year to €150,000 while the television executive showed no signs of slowing down. Dalkey native Pat and his partner Kathy, who is now an administrative director, were given a total of €201,975. They paid out €100,000 in a savings fund as well.

    For the last four decades, Newstalk anchor has been a fixture on-air and on our TVs and has hosted some of the highest-rated shows, including The Late Late Show, which he’s been hosting over ten years.

    In 2013, Kenny left RTE and took a stand for Newstalk Radio. Newstalk has recently poached him in a € 2 m contract with the Dalkey based media executive.

    He later negotiated with Virgin Media a lucrative deal for his eponymous current affairs series where he addresses the nation’s issues. It is known that he signed a € 500,00 contract with TV3 for the weekly show in front of it.

    Pat Kenny applies his seasoned form of reporting and incisive commentary to the coverage and real affairs of the day. The series combines deep analyses on current affairs, interviews of light entertainment news and live music of human interest.
